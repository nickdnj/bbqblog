Policies & Safety

Picnic & grill rules everyone must follow

Based on Monmouth County Parks regulations, Borough of Monmouth Beach ordinances, NFPA guidance, and Wharfside HOA policies.

General conduct & reservations
  • Reserve your grill slot in advance for groups over 12; gatherings of 50+ require Board approval 7 days prior.
  • Arrive on time and limit sessions to 2 hours when others are waiting.
  • No alcohol, controlled substances, glass bottles, or fireworks in the picnic area.
  • Keep music to conversational levels; amplification needs HOA sign-off.
Grill use & fire safety
  • Grills stay on their pedestals‚Äîdo not relocate or modify hardware.
  • Maintain a 10 ft safety perimeter from buildings, railings, and landscaping.
  • Use chimney starters, approved lighter fluid, or instant-light briquettes only.
  • Keep a sand bucket, spray bottle, or extinguisher within reach; never leave coals unattended.
Food safety & hygiene
  • Transport perishables on ice and keep raw and cooked items separate.
  • Follow USDA temperatures: 165¬∞F poultry, 160¬∞F ground meats, 145¬∞F whole cuts (3-minute rest), 140‚Äì145¬∞F seafood.
  • Use clean utensils and cutting boards; wash hands or use sanitizer frequently.
  • Leftovers must be chilled within 2 hours (1 hour if above 90¬∞F ambient).
Cleanup & ash disposal
  • Let coals burn down and cool fully. Scoop cold ash into the covered metal bins provided.
  • Brush grates, wipe shelves, and remove all trash, decorations, and food scraps.
  • Oil grates lightly after cleaning to prevent rust in the coastal climate.
  • Report damage, loose hardware, or corrosion via the HOA maintenance request.
Pool, beach & pet etiquette
  • Follow lifeguard directions; shower before using pool; no diving in shallow zones.
  • Pets must remain leashed, cleaned up after, and off beach areas March 15 ‚Äì Oct 1.
  • Keep smoke and music away from neighboring groups and residences.
  • Respect dunes and landscaping‚Äîno digging, staking, or attaching d√©cor.
Emergency plan
  • Dial 911 for emergencies, then notify the HOA office at 732-245-4057.
  • Fire extinguishers and first-aid kits are stored in the pool house lobby.
  • Know the location of ash bins, sand buckets, and hose bibs before lighting.
  • Complete an incident report within 24 hours for any injury or equipment issue.

Signature Experience

Wharfside Waterfront Showcase feast plan

A multi-course timeline for advanced hosts managing appetizers, mains, sides, and dessert across three grill zones.

Day-before prep

Finalize guest list, confirm reservations, marinate proteins, par-bake desserts, and print station signage.

T-minus 3 hours

Set up canopy, beverage station, and prep tables; stage coolers; lay out fire-start tools and ash bins.

T-minus 90 minutes

Light chimney starters, establish three-zone fire, preheat cast iron, and begin appetizers (oysters, clams, veggie skewers).

Main service

Reverse sear steaks in center zone, finish paella over hot coals, rotate veggies through medium zone, hold buns in warming basket.

Dessert & breakdown

Move crisps to cool zone, grill peaches last, extinguish coals responsibly, and launch cleanup checklist teams.
